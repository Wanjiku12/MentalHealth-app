About Tulia

Welcome to the Mental Wellness Platform — a safe, secure space designed to support your emotional and psychological well-being. This page allows you to access therapy sessions either through anonymous chat or video, without the need to share personal information.

You can check in daily to log your mood, write private journal entries, and set personal wellness goals. The platform offers self-help tools like breathing exercises and emotional check-ins, and all your activity remains confidential and encrypted.

We also believe in inclusivity and accessibility. You can choose your preferred language for a more comfortable experience.

Whether you're here to talk to a therapist or to quietly track your emotions, this platform is here to guide and support you — on your terms.
