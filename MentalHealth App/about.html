About Tulia
Tulia is a mental wellness app dedicated to fostering inclusion, understanding, and healing. We believe that everyone deserves access to mental health support in a language they understand and a space where they feel safe. With our multilingual feature, Tulia breaks language barriers and promotes open conversations around mental health. Our Mission
We believe everyone deserves access to mental health support. Tulia empowers individuals to track their well-being, connect with therapists, and find a safe space for self-expression. Our mission is to break the stigma, foster resilience, and make mental wellness accessible to all.
